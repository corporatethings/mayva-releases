## What's Mayva?

Mayva is a local-first personal agent for macOS. It reads your calendar, email, health (Whoop today; Apple Health, Oura, Fitbit on the roadmap), tasks, and goals and proposes the day's plan — drafts replies, schedules meetings, blocks deep-work windows, surfaces overdue commitments.

Everything runs on your machine. AI calls go to whichever provider you pick (Anthropic, OpenAI, Google Gemini, or local CLI variants); your data doesn't leave your laptop unless you explicitly connect a sync option.

Marketing site: **[mayva.ai](https://mayva.ai)**

## Auto-update

Mayva's in-app auto-updater polls this repo for new releases. When a new tag ships, the app prompts you to install on next launch.

If you want to skip a version, just dismiss the prompt — you'll be asked again on the next release.
